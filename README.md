# Trade Operations AI Platform

## What This Platform Does

This platform automates and intelligently manages your trade operations by catching discrepancies, identifying fraud, and routing exceptions to the right teams—all in real time.

It combines two powerful capabilities:

**Reconciliation Agent** - Automatically matches your purchase contracts, invoices, and shipping receipts to make sure everything aligns. It spots price differences, quantity mismatches, and suspicious patterns that could indicate fraud or invoice manipulation. When discrepancies are found, it calculates the financial impact so you know exactly what's at risk.

**Exception Triage Agent** - Monitors your shipments and financial instruments 24/7. When something goes wrong—a shipment is delayed, a required document is missing, a letter of credit is about to expire, or a vessel is staying in port too long—the platform automatically detects it, assesses the urgency, and routes it to the right team with a clear action plan.

## What Problems Does It Solve?

- **Lost visibility** into discrepancies and shipping exceptions until they become expensive problems
- **Manual reconciliation** that's slow, error-prone, and consumes valuable resources
- **Undetected fraud** where invoices are manipulated or documents are forged
- **Delayed response** to shipment delays and other time-sensitive issues because no one spotted them early
- **No clear ownership** of exceptions—they get lost between teams or no one knows who should handle them
- **Unknown financial exposure** because you can't quantify the impact of discrepancies in real time

## How to Use the Platform

When you log in, you'll see a unified dashboard showing:

**Home Dashboard** - A bird's-eye view of your trade operations: how many shipments are in transit, which letters of credit are active, which documents are pending, and critical alerts that need immediate attention.

**Reconciliation Workspace** - Upload or provide your contracts, invoices, and receipts. The platform automatically compares them and shows you exactly what matches and what doesn't. For each mismatch, it displays the financial impact and flags whether it looks suspicious.

**Exception Dashboard** - See all active exceptions in one place: delayed shipments, missing documents, upcoming LC expirations, and demurrage risks. Each exception has a color-coded urgency level (critical, high, medium) and a recommended action plan.

**Audit Trail** - A complete record of every decision the platform made: what was flagged as suspicious, why it was routed to a particular team, and when it was resolved. This is your compliance record.

**Alerts & Notifications** - See high-priority issues that need immediate attention, with summaries of what's wrong and why it matters.

**Settings** - Configure how the platform monitors your operations: how often it checks for exceptions, which teams it should notify, and what threshold triggers an alert.

## Key Features

**Real-Time Monitoring** - The platform continuously watches your shipments, documents, and contracts. No waiting for end-of-day or end-of-week reports.

**Intelligent Routing** - Exceptions are automatically sent to the right team with all the context they need. A shipment delay goes to Logistics. A missing document goes to Compliance. A forged invoice goes to Finance.

**Fraud Detection** - Uses multiple signals to spot suspicious patterns: price spikes, quantity mismatches, shipper-to-receiver inconsistencies, and document anomalies. Each exception gets a fraud risk score so you know how much to worry.

**Financial Impact** - See exactly how much each discrepancy or exception is costing you in potential exposure.

**Human Approval Loop** - The platform makes recommendations but lets your teams make final decisions. Critical actions require manual approval before they're executed.

**Complete Audit Trail** - Every action is logged with timestamps and decision rationale, making compliance audits easy.

## Getting Started

1. **Provide Your Data** - Connect your systems or manually upload contracts, invoices, and shipping documents.
2. **Set Your Preferences** - Configure which shipments to monitor, which thresholds should trigger alerts, and which teams should be notified.
3. **Monitor & Respond** - The platform does the heavy lifting. Your teams focus on resolving exceptions rather than finding them.

## Who Benefits

- **Finance Teams** - Catch invoice fraud and discrepancies before they hit the books
- **Logistics Teams** - Know immediately when shipments are delayed or at risk of demurrage charges
- **Compliance Teams** - Maintain complete audit trails for regulatory requirements
- **Operations Managers** - Get a unified view of all trade operations and their health
- **Executives** - Understand your financial exposure and operational risks in real time

## Why This Matters

Trade operations involve hundreds of documents, multiple parties, and tight timelines. Even small discrepancies compound quickly. A single missed letter of credit expiration could trigger penalties. An overlooked invoice fraud could hit your profit margins hard. A delayed shipment announcement that arrives too late costs you demurrage fees.

This platform transforms trade operations from reactive (catching problems after they hurt) to proactive (spotting and addressing them before they happen).
